Roblox Creator Documentation

2026 Unofficial Strategy Guide for Dominist Studios

Section 1: The "Public" Strategy

To allow random players to find your game via search or direct links, your experience must be set to Public. As of early 2026, accounts must meet at least one trust requirement.

Trust Requirements

  • ID Verification: Valid government-issued ID.
  • Verified Purchaser: Real-currency purchase since Jan 1, 2025.
  • Established Status: History of DevEx or high milestones.

Steps to Publish

  1. Open Roblox Studio and File > Publish.
  2. Go to the Creator Dashboard.
  3. Complete the Content Maturity Questionnaire.
  4. Under Permissions, toggle to Public and Save.

Section 2: The "No-Verify" Workaround

If you prefer not to verify, you can manually authorize players as "Connections." This limits access only to those you specifically invite.

Authorized Access Setup

  • Friendship: Must be friends on Roblox first.
  • Collaborate: Use the button in the top right of Studio.
  • Permissions: Search the username and set to Play.
