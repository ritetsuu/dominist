Bot Functions and Commands

📊 Points & Economy

/checkpoints [@user]: Displays the current balance of the mentioned user. If no user is mentioned, it shows your own balance.

⚙️ Utility & Server Stats

Tools for monitoring the bot and your community.

  • /uptime: Shows exactly how many days, hours, and minutes the bot has been running since its last restart.
  • /oldestusers : Lists the first members to ever join the server (up to 25). Great for identifying veteran members.
  • /serverinfo: Displays a summary of the server, including member count and creation date.
  • /avatar [@user]: Pulls the full-size profile picture of any member.

🎲 Fun & Randomization
  • /random: Randomly selects one human member from the server. Useful for giveaways or picking "Member of the Day."
  • /randomnumber (min) (max): Generates a random integer between the two numbers provided.
